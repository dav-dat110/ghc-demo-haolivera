Blockquote

I believe that our final project will benefit greatly from this technique. With our topic being such a heavy and under-researched area, I think quotes or statistics would be very insightful to bring the reader’s attention to. With various themes and colors, the blockquote technique can be perfect for important information.

Links

Inserting links into an html document or website can be very useful, but also hurtful. Positively, websites can prevent authors from getting off topic of the main investigation, and instead invites readers who are interested to find out more of a topic or data. It also produces and exact citation to a source of information. Counteractively, in the same way the links can help, they can prove to be a disadvantage as the author can not expand as much on the topic as they should, instead leaving vital information out of the investigation. In our final project, I think linking websites might prove useful to different case studies, statistics, or articles regarding LGBTQ+ mental health and suicide rates, but we will have to be cautious to not get off topic nor accidentally omit information.
